Green seedless grapes can help you meet your vitamins A, C and K needs. One cup of grapes provides 100 international units of vitamin A, 4.8 milligrams of vitamin C and 22 micrograms of vitamin K. Vitamin A helps your body fight off infection by boosting your immune system. Vitamin C is important for healthy gums and muscles and also helps wounds to heal. And finally, vitamin K is essential for blood clotting. The best way to get your daily Vitamin A is to eat at least 1 cup of grapes per day.
Benefits
  • Grapes provide a good amount of antioxidants. They contain great amounts of lycopene which is a cancer preventive.
  • Good for vision. Eating grapes daily improves eyesight.
Storage and Uses
  • Wash the grapes under cool, running water. Pat them dry and store it in the refrigerator.Rinse well and eat them right off the vine for a healthy, refreshing snack any time of day.
  • You can also freeze them and toss them in drinks.
